Creative thinking is highly prized across many domains but only formally taught to a few (e.g. designers and writers). As a consequence we face constant challenges when making creative decisions, slowing progress and exposing companies to significant risk. My work focuses on how we might make creative decisions in the future, designing faster and at higher quality than we do today. This research spans design, society and artificial intelligence, with the following core topics:
